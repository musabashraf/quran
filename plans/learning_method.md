# Part 1: Pattern Training

This is a guide for instructors or self-learners. The following learning method is scientifically designed and tested for deep learning and should be strictly followed.

---

## 📋 Course Prerequisites & Guidelines

### Eligibility
* **Arabic Reading:** The student should already know how to read Arabic.

### Methodology
* **Physical Actions (Total Physical Response):** This must be done with hand-pointing actions.
* **Collective Learning:** All students repeat the same together loudly.
* **Objective:** The goal is to create automated muscle memory for the **8 Arabic pronouns** (excluding feminine forms for now).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Feminine forms should be strictly avoided at this stage.** They will be taught later to avoid cognitive overload.

> [!TIP]
> Include cartoons/illustrations of the hand actions for all 8 pronouns in the teaching materials.

---

## 📖 Chapter 3: Pronouns (الضَّمِير)

### 🔄 Practice Routine
1. **Verbs & Action (10x):** Repeat the past and present verbs together of **فَعَلَ** (*fa'ala*) for all 8 pronouns 10 times with actions and memorize.
2. **Verbs, Action & Uttered Meaning (5x):** Instructor explains the meaning. Everyone repeats the Arabic words and meanings with actions 5 times.
3. **Verbs, Action & Silent Meaning (10x):** Repeat the Arabic words and actions while thinking the meanings (without uttering them) 10 times.

*Ensure this is solid and clear.*
