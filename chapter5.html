فِعْل مُضَارِع (Present Verbs)

  • • Identify who is performing the action now or in the future.
  • • These "Subject" markers appear as prefixes (at the start) and sometimes suffixes.
  • • Feminine forms of the verbs are not included in the main table for ease of learning. Present feminine forms are rare in the Quran.

Quranic Examples

You All Do (تَ...ونَ)

لَا أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ (109:2)

I do not worship what **you all worship**

നിങ്ങൾ ആരാധിക്കുന്നതിനെ ഞാൻ ആരാധിക്കുന്നില്ല

I Do (أَ)

لَا أَعْبُدُ مَا تَعْبُدُونَ (109:2)

I do not **worship** what you all worship

നിങ്ങൾ ആരാധിക്കുന്നതിനെ ഞാൻ ആരാധിക്കുന്നില്ല

He Does (يَ)

يَعْلَمُ مَا بَيْنَ أَيْدِيهِمْ (2:255)

**He knows** what is before them

അവരുടെ മുമ്പിലുള്ളത് അവൻ അറിയുന്നു

Chapter 5 Learning Instructions
📋 Prerequisites & Guidelines
  • Eligibility: The student should already know how to read Arabic.
  • Methodology: This must be done with hand-pointing actions. All students repeat together loudly.
  • Objective: To create an automated muscle memory for the 8 Arabic pronouns in present tense verbs (excluding feminine forms for now).
Important Note

Feminine forms should be strictly avoided at this stage. They will be taught in later chapters to avoid cognitive overload.

🔄 1. Base Verb Practice Routine (فَعَلَ -> يَفْعَلُ)
1
Verb & Action Only (10x)

Repeat the present verbs of فَعَلَ for all 8 pronouns 10 times with hand actions and memorize.

2
Arabic + Action + Uttering Meaning (5x)

Instructor explains the meaning. Everyone repeats the Arabic verb and the meaning with actions 5 times.

3
Arabic + Action + Thinking Meaning (10x)

Repeat the Arabic verb and action while thinking of the meaning silently (without uttering it) 10 times.

🔄 2. Practice with 5 Additional Verbs

Apply the routine to the following 5 sound verbs in their present tense forms:

Arabic Transliteration Meaning
يَكْتُبُ yaktubu He writes / is writing
يَجْلِسُ yajlisu He sits / is sitting
يَضْرِبُ yadribu He hits / is hitting
يَشْرَبُ yashrabu He drinks / is drinking
يَدْرُسُ yadrusu He studies / is studying

For each verb, follow this sequence:

A
10x with hand action.
B
5x with hand action and uttering the meaning.
C
10x with hand action and thinking the meaning (silent).
