Identifying Particles (حُرُوف)
Preposition (حَرْف جَرّ)

These particles specifically change the Ism after them to the Majroor state (ending with 'i').

Impact Changes Ending
Example مِنَ اللَّهِ (From Allah)
Other Particles (حُرُوف أُخْرَى)

Not all particles are prepositions. Some like 'Wa' (and) or 'Inna' (indeed) have different effects.

Impact Meaning Only
Example وَاللَّهُ (And Allah)
