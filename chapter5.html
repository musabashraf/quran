Quran Root Method

Chapter 5

The 14 Verb Endings (Past Tense) (تَصْرِيف الْمَاضِي)

  • • Identify who performed the action (I did, You did, They did).
  • • These "Subject" endings attach to the end of Past Tense (Madi) verbs.
  • • Unlike Chapter 4, these tell us the Subject (Hero), not the object.

The "Sukun" Secret

Notice that for I, You, and We, the last letter of the root always has a Sukun (a stop).

نَصَرْ تَ
Stop + TA = You did
Who vs Whom
Subject (Chapter 5)

Tells us **WHO** did it.

نَصَرْتَ (You helped)
Object (Chapter 4)

Tells us **WHOM** it happened to.

نَصَرَكَ (He helped you)

Quranic Examples

You Did (تَ)

وَرَأَيْتَ النَّاسَ (110:2)

And **you saw** the people

ജനങ്ങൾ ... നീ കാണുകയും ചെയ്താൽ

You All Did (تُمْ)

وَلَا أَنَا عَابِدٌ مَا عَبَدْتُمْ (109:4)

Nor am I a worshipper of what **you all worshipped**

നിങ്ങൾ ആരാധിച്ചതിനെ ഞാൻ ആരാധിക്കുന്നവനല്ല

We Did (نَا)

لَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا الْإِنْسَانَ (95:4)

Surely **We created** man

തീർച്ചയായും നാം മനുഷ്യനെ സൃഷ്ടിച്ചു
