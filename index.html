About the Course

The objective of this course is to enable anyone who can read the Quran to understand it directly without the need for any translation by learning through the Root Method.

Simplified Grammar

We have designed this syllabus to teach Quranic grammar in the easiest manner possible, focusing on patterns that cover the majority of the Quranic text.

The Root Method

By mastering 3-letter roots and their basic transformations, you can unlock the meaning of thousands of words without memorizing endless vocabulary lists.

Syllabus Overview

1
Word Classification

Learn how every word in the Quran is either an Ism (Name), Fil (Verb), or Huroof (Particle). This foundation is key to all further learning.

2
The 5 Basic Forms

Understand the fundamental DNA of Arabic grammar through five core transformations that apply to almost any 3-letter root word.

3
Mudari Forms & Atayna

Master the present and future tense using the simple 'Atayna' prefix method to identify the subject of any action.
