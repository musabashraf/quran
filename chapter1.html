Quran Root Method

Chapter 1:
Word Classification

Every single word in the Quran falls into one of these three simple categories.

Ism

اِسْم
Nouns & Names

Words that point to a person, place, thing, or quality. They are NOT tied to time (past/present/future).

Allah اللَّه
Messenger رَسُول
Book كِتَاب
Paradise جَنَّة
Fire نَار

Fil

فِعْل
Verbs & Actions

Words that describe an action and ARE tied to a specific time (Past, Present, or Command).

He said قَالَ
He created خَلَقَ
He knew عَلِمَ
Say! قُلْ
He made جَعَلَ

Huroof

حَرْف
Particles

Words that don't give a complete meaning on their own unless they are joined with an Ism or a Fil.

In فِي
From مِنْ
On عَلَى
And وَ
To إِلَى
Quick Tip

Think of **Ism** as the "What" (people, things), **Fil** as the "When" (actions happening in time), and **Huroof** as the "Glue" (connecting everything together).
