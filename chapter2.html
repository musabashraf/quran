Quran Root Method

Chapter 2: The 5 Basic Forms

Every 3-letter root in the Quran follows these five fundamental transformations.

Grammar Form Core Pattern Example Root Form Explanation
مَاضِي
Past
فَعَلَ
He did
كَتَبَ
"He wrote"

Describes an action that has already been completed.
مُضَارِع
Present
يَفْعَلُ
He does
يَكْتُبُ
"He writes"

Describes an action currently happening or that will happen.
أَمْر
Command
اِفْعَلْ
Do!
اُكْتُبْ
"Write!"

An instruction or order given directly to someone.

Ism Fail
فَاعِل
One who does
كَاتِب

The person or entity performing the action.

Ism Mafool
مَفْعُول
Receiver of action
مَكْتُوب
"Written"

The person or entity the action is performed upon.

Quranic Examples

Past
مَاضِي

خَلَقَ الْإِنْسَانَ

"He created man" (96:2)

نَصَرَكَ اللَّهُ

"Allah helped you" (48:3)

قَالَ لَهُ صَاحِبُهُ

"His companion said to him" (18:37)

Present
مُضَارِع

يَعْلَمُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ

"He knows what is in the heavens" (3:29)

يَنْصُرُ مَنْ يَشَاءُ

"He helps whom He wills" (30:5)

يَقُولُ الْإِنْسَانُ

"Man will say" (75:10)

Command
أَمْر

اقْرَأْ بِاسْمِ رَبِّكَ

"Read in the name of your Lord" (96:1)

قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ

"Say, He is Allah, One" (112:1)

اُعْبُدُوا اللَّهَ

"Worship Allah" (2:21)

Ism Fail
اِسْم فَاعِل

أَنَا خَالِقٌ بَشَرًا

"I am a creator of a human" (38:71)

أَلَيْسَ اللَّهُ بِعَالِمٍ

"Is not Allah most knowing?" (29:10)

اللَّهُ غَالِبٌ عَلَى أَمْرِهِ

"Allah is predominant over His affair" (12:21)

Ism Mafool

فِي كِتَابٍ مَسْطُورٍ

ذَلِكَ يَوْمٌ مَشْهُودٌ

"That is a day witnessed" (11:103)

جَعَلَهُ هَبَاءً مَنْثُورًا

"He made it scattered dust" (25:23)
