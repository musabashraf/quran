نَصَائِحُ النَّحْوِ (Grammar Pro-Tips)

1. The "Human" Ending

The ـُونَ and ـِينَ endings are almost exclusively used for Humans (Believers, Muslims, Mushrikeen). If the plural is for things (like trees or pens), it will use the "Sound Feminine" or "Broken" form.

2. "Broken" is "She"

In Arabic, Broken plurals for Non-Humans are treated as Singular Feminine. This is why you see هَذِهِ أَنْهَار (This is rivers) using the feminine "this".
