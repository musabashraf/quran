# Part 1: Pattern Training

This is a guide for instructors or self-learners. The following learning method is scientifically designed and tested for deep learning and should be strictly followed.

---

## 📋 Course Prerequisites & Guidelines

### Eligibility
* **Arabic Reading:** The student should already know how to read Arabic.

### Methodology
* **Physical Actions (Total Physical Response):** This must be done with hand-pointing actions.
* **Collective Learning:** All students repeat the same together loudly.
* **Objective:** The goal is to create automated muscle memory for the **8 Arabic pronouns** (excluding feminine forms for now).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Feminine forms should be strictly avoided at this stage.** They will be taught later to avoid cognitive overload.

> [!TIP]
> Include cartoons/illustrations of the hand actions for all 8 pronouns in the teaching materials.

---

## 📖 Chapter 3: Pronouns

### 🔄 Practice Routine
1. **Pronoun & Action (20x):** Repeat the 8 pronouns with actions 20 times and memorize.
2. **Pronoun, Action & Uttered Meaning (10x):** Repeat the Arabic word, the corresponding action, and utter its meaning 20 times.
3. **Pronoun, Action & Silent Meaning (20x):** Repeat the Arabic word and action while thinking the meaning (without uttering it) 20 times.

*Ensure that this foundation is solid and clear before moving forward.*

### 🙋‍♂️ Pronouns & Hand Actions Reference

| Arabic | Transliteration | Meaning | Hand Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **هُوَ** | *hua* | He / Him (singular) | Single finger pointing to the right |
| **هُمَا** | *huma* | They (dual) | Two fingers pointing to the right |
| **هُمْ** | *hum* | They (plural) | Palm pointing to the right |
| **أَنْتَ** | *anta* | You (singular) | Single finger pointing straight ahead |
| **أَنْتُمَا** | *antuma* | You (dual) | Two fingers pointing straight ahead |
| **أَنْتُمْ** | *antum* | You (plural) | Palm pointing straight ahead |
| **أَنَا** | *ana* | I / Me | Single finger pointing to the chest |
| **نَحْنُ** | *nahnu* | We / Us | Palm pointing to the chest |

## 📖 Chapter 4: Past Verbs

### 🔄 Practice Routine
1. **Verb & Action (10x):** Repeat the past verbs of **فَعَلَ** (*fa'ala*) for all 8 pronouns 10 times with actions and memorize.
2. **Verb, Action & Uttered Meaning (5x):** Instructor explains the meaning. Everyone repeats the Arabic word and the meaning with actions 5 times.
3. **Verb, Action & Silent Meaning (10x):** Repeat the Arabic word and action while thinking the meaning (without uttering it) 10 times.

*Ensure this is solid and clear before proceeding.*
