Part1. Pattern training. 

This is a guide for instructors. The following teaching method is scientifically crafted for deep learning and should be strictly followed.

Eligibility of students: The student should already know how to read arabic.

Methodology: This should be done as a chorus group of students with hand pointing action. All students repeating the same loudly. The goal is to create an automated muscle memory for the 8 arabic pronouns without feminine forms.

Feminine forms should be strictly avoided now. It will be tought later.

(Include cartoons of the actions for all 8 pronouns)

Chapter1: Pronouns

Let them repeat the 8 pronouns with action 20 times and memorize. 
Then do the same with the arabic word, action and uttering meaning 20 times. 
Then do the same with arabic word, thinking the meaning(not uttering) and action 20 times.

Make sure that this is solid clear.

hua - single finger pointing right
huma - two fingers right
hum - palm pointing right

anta - single finger pointing straight
antuma - two fingers pointing straight
antum - palm pointing straight

ana - single finter pointing to chest
nahnu - palm pointing to chest


Chapter 2: Past verbs

Let them repeat past verbs of fa'ala for all 8 pronouns, 20 times with action and memorize
Then do the same with the arabic word, action and uttering meaning 20 times. 
Then do the same with arabic word, thinking the meaning(not uttering) and action 20 times.

Make sure that this is solid clear.

Then do for 5 examples kataba, jalasa, laraba, shariba, darasa. 
first 10 times with action 
next 10 times with action and uttering the meaning 
next 10 times with action and thinking the meaning

Past verbs with 
hua - fa'ala
huma - fa'alaa
hum - fa'alu

anta - fa'alta
antuma - fa'altuma
